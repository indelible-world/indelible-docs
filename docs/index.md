# Welcome to indelible.world

indelible.world is building a protocol for establishing verifiable, on-chain authorship of digital content, without relying on any centralized platform, server, or authority.

---

## The Problem

The internet has no native concept of authorship. Anyone can copy and republish content, backdate a post, or claim credit for work they didn't create. Traditional timestamps are issued by servers that can be tampered with or taken offline. Existing attribution systems depend on platforms that can de-platform users, alter records, or simply disappear.

indelible.world solves this by anchoring authorship to a public blockchain, a ledger that is immutable, globally replicated, and controlled by no single party.
